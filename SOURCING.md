# Sourcing & monetization plan

Researched June 18, 2026 (verified against live vendor pages). How to populate the tienda with real product and monetize, since we launch with almost no inventory. **Meta-finding: there is no turnkey API-dropship for Colombian food or crafts.** The real model is wholesale/bulk from US-based importers (mostly Florida), B2B marketplaces (Faire, net-60) for cash-flow cushion, plus two true no-inventory plays: private-label coffee and print-on-demand fanwear. Confirm every MOQ/margin by direct contact; most are quote-on-request.

## Coffee
- **Cordialsa USA** (cordialsausa.com) — the key name. Grupo Nutresa's official US distributor: Café Sello Rojo (Colombia's #1 brand) + Colcafé, 5 US warehouses (TX/CA/FL/NJ/MD). Brand-recognized by the diaspora. Call for MOQ. info@cordialsausa.com.
- **ColombianCoffee.us** (Miami, also on Faire) — Sello Rojo, Águila Roja, La Bastilla + own-label Supremo/Excelso. Inquiry-only pricing.
- Green beans if we ever roast: Genuine Origin (65-lb boxes, $6.11-$11/lb, verified), Andina Distributors (private-label program).
- Juan Valdez at grocery scale: Green Coffee Company holds US supermarket rights (institutional scale; revisit at volume).

## Private-label coffee (true no-inventory)
- **Roastify** (roastify.app) — LEAD. Named Colombian single-origin, blind packaging, ~$8.25-$12/12oz, roast-to-ship 1-5 days, native Shopify app. Confirm single-origin SKU + blind packing slip + request a sample.
- **Old Chicago Coffee** — cheap fallback ($10/mo, free custom labels, blind via generic return address) but WooCommerce-first and Colombia only in blends.
- **Dripshipper** — most turnkey Shopify flow, dedicated Colombia page, but worst unit economics (~$14 landed + $30+/mo floor).

## Snacks & sweets (Florida importers, B2B portals)
- **Colombina USA** (colombinaus.com) — the actual maker of Bon Bon Bum, wafers, Coffee Delight; has a bulk + private-label B2B program. Miami.
- **JETa Imports** (jeta-imports.com) — same-day FL shipping before 3pm ET; Alpina (arequipe), Colombina, bocadillo, full mekato candy line. Strong for low inventory.
- **Fedenico** (fedenico.com) — Su Sabor house brand (bocadillo, obleas), Yupi, Postobón/Colombiana soda; serves 20+ states; Pompano Beach.
- **East Coast Food Distributors** (NH) — bocadillo lines, good Northeast logistics. **Titos** (Miami) — Chocoramo, Jet, arequipe.
- Amigo Foods (Doral) had the clearest public wholesale tiers (30-40% off $500-$1,000+) but **wholesale likely closed as of mid-2026; confirm.**

## Crafts (Wayuu, sombrero vueltiao) — all ship from Colombia, 1-2 wk lead, 2x-4x markup
- **Lombia + Co.** (lombia.co) — clearest terms: MOQ 10, 50% off retail (built-in 2x). FedEx 2-3 days. On Faire.
- **One Thread Collective** (onethreadcollective.org) — US nonprofit, MOQ 5, 100% of profit to Wayuu projects (great story). On Faire.
- **Best of Colombia** (bestofcolombia.com) — broadest: Wayuu + sombrero vueltiao (~$135-145 retail) + hammocks. La Guajira, 2-3 day US ship.
- Avoid **PACTUS / pwayuu.com** — China-based intermediary, not artisan-direct.

## Fan gear — READ THE LICENSING LINE
- **Official Colombia / World Cup 2026 jerseys are OFF-LIMITS.** adidas-designed, FCF/FIFA licensed; only Fanatics + FIFA Store can sell them. Counterfeits = CBP seizures ($6M seized in Houston), criminal cases, and **frozen payment gateways**. Hard no.
- **Safe to sell:** flags + generic Colombia-colors fanwear + our own POD designs with NO FIFA/FCF/adidas marks and no player names/numbers. This matches our existing "unofficial fanwear" framing (keep the jersey mark-free).
- **Flags Importer** (flagsimporter.com) — transparent: 3x5 flags $2.80, stick flags $0.60-1.00, hand fans $3.95+. US ship, retail 3.5x-5x. Open now.
- Original fanwear via **Printful/Printify** POD (below).

## Print-on-demand merch
- **Printful** (free plan) — LEAD for us: single-factory color consistency (protects tricolor/dark-fútbol designs), best embroidery + branded inserts/labels, US fulfillment. Flip to Growth ($24.99/mo) past ~$12k/yr.
- **Printify** — cost lever on proven high-volume tees (~$4/shirt cheaper) but quality varies by provider.
- **Gelato** — for the multi-country network later (local-to-customer routing). **Apliiq** — premium sewn-label fanwear capsule.
- Keep "artisan-direct" claims OFF POD blanks; those claims belong only to the artesanía line.

## La Caja Mecato (monthly snack/goodie box)
- Source the authentic core from JETa + Colombina USA + Fedenico (Su Sabor, Postobón); rotating novelty via Faire/Abound.
- **Self-kit in-house at launch** (3PL monthly minimums ~$275-517 don't pay off until a few hundred boxes/mo), then ShipMonk or Fulfillrite.
- Recurring billing via **Subbly or Shopify+ReCharge** to avoid Cratejoy marketplace's **15%-per-renewal** fee (use Cratejoy only for discovery).
- Price tiers ~**$18 mini / $30-35 standard** to sit between My Colombian Box ($10-12, cheap/light brand) and premium rotating boxes (Pachanga/SnackCrate/Universal Yums ~$35). Our edge: Colombia-deep + brand-rich (El Paisa voice, QR-to-article tie-ins, World Cup).
- Compliance: don't open/repackage sealed items (stays "reseller," not "manufacturer"); imported stock must carry US-compliant labels; use Stripe Tax (candy/soda often taxable even where grocery is exempt); keep everything shelf-stable.

## Amazon Associates (the filler, not the core)
- **Grocery/Gourmet food pays just 1%** — our most on-brand category is Amazon's worst. Fan gear/apparel ~3-4%, home/kitchen 3-4.5%, books 4.5%, beauty up to 10%. So Amazon is for the *adjacent* purchases; our own store/private-label is where coffee+food margin lives.
- Link format: `amazon.com/dp/ASIN/?tag=ourtag-20`. **SiteStripe is text-only since Dec 2023** (product images now require PA-API). Cookie: **24h click + 89-day cart** (last-click wins). Must hit **3 sales in 180 days** to keep the account.
- **OneLink** (free JS snippet) geo-routes our global diaspora clicks to their local Amazon and credits us — turn it on day one.
- Disclosure (required, verbatim): "As an Amazon Associate I earn from qualifying purchases." Now allowed in opted-in email (since Mar 2024), never in PDFs. Site-wide disclosure is now live in our footer.

## B2B marketplaces
- **Faire** (faire.com) — net-60, free returns on first order per brand, low MOQs. Best launch cash cushion. Colombian coffee + Wayuu brands already live there.
- **Mable** (meetmable.com) — net-45, grocery/CPG-specific. Abound folded into Carro; Tundra is defunct.

## Prioritized launch path
1. Cordialsa USA distributor account (Sello Rojo + Colcafé).
2. Free Faire account; stock coffee + Wayuu on net-60 (ColombianCoffee.us, Lombia+Co., One Thread Collective).
3. B2B accounts: JETa + Colombina USA + Fedenico (snacks).
4. Roastify for our own zero-inventory coffee brand (confirm single-origin + blind).
5. Flags Importer + Printful/Printify for legal fanwear. Never official jerseys.
6. Lombia+Co. + Best of Colombia for crafts; price in 1-2 wk lead times.
7. Amazon Associates + OneLink for the long tail and global clicks.
